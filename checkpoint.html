Bella Vista Bistro,Benvenuti da Chef Youssef Ben Chabene

A taste of Italy, right in your neighborhood.

Appetizers

  • Truffle Arancini - Crispy, golden risotto balls stuffed with melted mozzarella and infused with black truffle oil.
  • Caprese Skewers - Vine-ripened cherry tomatoes, fresh bocconcini mozzarella, and basil leaves drizzled with extra virgin olive oil.
  • Spinach & Artichoke Dip - A warm, creamy blend of spinach, artichokes, and parmesan, served with toasted flatbread.

Main Courses

  • Tuscan Ribeye Steak - Fire-grilled ribeye rubbed with rosemary and garlic, served alongside roasted fingerling potatoes.
  • Handcrafted Lasagna - Layers of fresh pasta, rich bolognese sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted mozzarella baked to perfection.
  • Shrimp Scampi Pasta - Succulent shrimp sautéed in a garlic, white wine, and butter sauce, tossed with fresh linguine.

Desserts

  • Signature Tiramisu - Espresso-soaked ladyfingers layered with a whipped mascarpone cream and dusted with dark cocoa powder.
  • Vanilla Bean Panna Cotta - A silky, chilled Italian custard topped with a vibrant mixed berry coulis.
  • Warm Apple Crostata - A rustic, flaky pastry tart filled with spiced apples, served with a drizzle of caramel sauce.
